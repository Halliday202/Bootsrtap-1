Logitech G502x

The G502 X is a gaming mouse that is part of Logitech's legendary G502 lineage. It is a lightweight and ergonomic mouse that is designed for both gaming and productivity. The G502 X features a HERO 25K sensor, which offers high precision and accuracy. It also has LIGHTFORCE hybrid optical-mechanical switches, which provide a fast and responsive feel. The G502 X is available in both black and white colorways.

Lenovo Legion 9i

The Lenovo Legion 9i is a high-performance gaming laptop designed for demanding gamers and content creators. It features a powerful Intel Core i9 processor and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090 Laptop GPU, ensuring smooth gameplay and efficient multitasking. The laptop boasts a stunning 16-inch 3.2K Mini-LED display with a 165Hz refresh rate, delivering vibrant visuals and fast response times. Additionally, it comes with a liquid cooling system to keep the components cool under heavy load.

Alienware AW3225QF OLED Curved 4k

The Alienware AW3225QF is a high-end 32-inch 4K QD-OLED gaming monitor with a 240Hz refresh rate. It offers exceptional image quality with deep blacks, vibrant colors, and fast response times. The QD-OLED technology provides superior color accuracy and wider color gamut compared to traditional OLED panels. The monitor also features NVIDIA G-SYNC Compatible technology for smooth gameplay and minimal tearing.  

Google Pixel 9 Pro

The Google Pixel 9 Pro is a premium smartphone with a focus on photography and AI-powered features. It boasts a powerful triple rear camera system, including a 50MP main camera, a 48MP telephoto lens, and a 48MP ultrawide lens. The phone also features a 42MP front-facing camera for high-quality selfies.   Other notable features include a 6.3-inch Super Actua display with a 120Hz refresh rate, a powerful processor, and a long-lasting battery. The Pixel 9 Pro runs on the latest version of Android and offers a seamless user experience.

Steam Deck

The Steam Deck is a powerful handheld gaming computer developed by Valve. It allows you to play your entire Steam library on the go, offering a console-like experience with full-sized controls, trackpads, and gyro aiming. Powered by a custom AMD APU, it can run the latest AAA games and is highly customizable with SteamOS 3.0.
